# KMK Firmware for Couch Clicker Atomic

## Installing KMK Firmware

* [Install CircuitPython](https://learn.adafruit.com/welcome-to-circuitpython/installing-circuitpython) on the XIAO board.

* Copy the entire contents of [this folder](.) into the CircuitPython drive mounted from the XIAO board.

* Optionally, rename the CircuitPython volume to 'CLICKER'
